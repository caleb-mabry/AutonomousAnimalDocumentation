The Autonomous Animal

A bot that tracks the prices of turnips in all of your friends villages throughout the week. Sell when the prices are high and buy when the prices are low.

This graph is live!

 
When inviting the bot to your server, make sure to keep it in its own channel.
!report <number>

Report a price for the current day

!report none

Remove a price for the current day

!report <number> <Day>-<am or pm>

Report a price for a specific day
